All About the Alumni

who we are

Lakipia Air Base Primary School Alumni Association brings together members of the Laikipia Air Base (LAB) fraternity who were brought up in LAB and went through LAB schools.

MOTTO

Stronger Together

Mission

We endeavour to enrich lifelong relationships among the alumni body and the schools.

Vision

To be an alumni association that fosters a sense of togetherness while instilling in its members a lifelong commitment to our alma mater and the community.

Value

    • Tradition: We believe in Laikipia Air Base schools. We cherish the rich and vibrant history and work to enhance its reputation
    • Integrity : We keep our promises. Our reputation rests on honesty, fairness and treating everyone with respect.
    • Service : We go the extra mile. We provide the highest levels of service to our members, growing Alumni community and the schools.
    • Quality: We expect to be judged by standards of excellence in everything we do.
    • Diversity: We value diversity. We embrace inclusion in everything we do.

Vision

    • We establish and consolidate a network of contacts through which Laikipia Air Base alumni can always keep in touch with each other. We provide a platform for Alumni across different generations to network and build meaningful relationships.
    • We provide a platform for members of the Alumni association to share knowledge and talents for the furtherance of fellowship.
    • We provide a platform for members of the association to generate wealth by organizing seminars on investment and business opportunities.
    We provide a platform for members of the association to market their businesses and share job opportunities.
    • We undertake various CSR activities that add value to the community and generate positive impact.
    • We provide material and financial support and other related aid to members of the association as guided by our group Constitution.
